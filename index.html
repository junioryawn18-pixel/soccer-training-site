Soccer Training

This website is about basic soccer training and how players can improve.

Why Soccer Training Matters

Training helps players get better at skills like passing, shooting, and running.

It also helps with fitness and staying healthy.

Basic Skills

  • Passing
  • Dribbling
  • Shooting
  • Running

Weekly Practice Plan

  1. Practice with the ball
  2. Do running exercises
  3. Work on shooting
  4. Rest one day
